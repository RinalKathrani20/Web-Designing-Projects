Why India?

India is a vast and diverse country located in South Asia. It has a rich history dating back thousands of years and is home to one of the world's oldest civilizations. India is known for unity in diversity, where people of many cultures, languages, and religions live together peacefully. The country has made progress in science, education, technology, and space research while preserving strong family values and traditions.
